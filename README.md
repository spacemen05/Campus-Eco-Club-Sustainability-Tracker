The Campus Eco-Club Sustainability Tracker is a system created to help the Eco-Club manage their 
environmental activities in a more organized and digital way. The main idea is to make it easier for 
students to join eco-friendly events such as community cleanup days, zero waste festivals and more, 
by this they can upload proof of recycling photos or videos, by doing so they also record their 
recycling activity and keep track of how much they had contributed. 
There are four primary users are involved in this project the Admin, Volunteer, and Student 
Member and Event Organiser. 
Depending on their roles each of the users collaborate with the system in different ways. 
Admin ensures the systems are accurate, organized and has a meaningful sustainability in tracking. 
Admins have the access to managing the users accounts, updating it and to verify data of the users. 
Update info about the events for the participations, upload educational materials and keep the 
platform tidy by removing expired events which are no more in need. 

The Event Organiser oversees the logistics of these activities; they are responsible for creating and 
scheduling new events, managing participant slots, and coordinating details to ensure successful 
execution. 
 
Volunteer and Student Members can register, log in, look at upcoming events, sign up for events 
and contribute to recycling logs. They also can earn eco-points by participating in events. These 
points eventually will be linted in the leaderboard, which will encourage more students to continue 
their involvement in projects related to sustainability.
